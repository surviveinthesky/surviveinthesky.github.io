About the Game

Survive in the Sky drops you onto a chain of floating islands with limited resources. Explore, gather materials, craft gear, and expand your base; face environmental hazards and other players, and work together or compete to control islands.

Key Features

  • Dynamic floating island maps and biomes
  • Resource gathering, crafting and base building
  • PvE encounters — monsters, bosses and events
  • Optional PvP and trading between players
  • Regular updates and seasonal content

How to Play

  • Spawn on a starter island and gather wood, stone, and food.
  • Build bridges to reach neighboring islands.
  • Upgrade tools and craft defensive structures.
  • Complete island challenges to unlock special resources.

Tips

  • Use bridges sparingly — they’re expensive but strategic.
  • Form alliances to take down strong bosses.
  • Keep a stash of supplies on multiple islands.
